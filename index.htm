ACHIEVEMENTS

MY ACHIEVEMENTS

Hard work and dedication are the only mantras in the journey to success. Without being passionate and having the willingness to work hard, no one can achieve success. Desire is the starting point of all achievements, not a hope, not a wish, but a keen pulsating desire that transcends everything."Thanks to the small goals I had set along the way and the schedule I had created, I was able to get my certification in a year, and with that certification I was able to get a promotion."

PERFORMANCES

MY PERFORMANCES

Being relaxed, at peace with yourself, confident, emotionally neutral, loose, and free-floating – these are the keys to successful performance in almost everything. I have been giving both vocal and violin concerts from a young age. I have given concerts in some of the well reputed and esteemed platforms like the Iskcon temple , Indian institute of world culture , Bangalore Ganaya Samaja and some prestigious and eminent Facebook pages like Mani Krishna Swami Academy , Natana Tarangini , Music of Madras , Music of Bangalore. “All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them."

TUTILAGE

Vid. V Nalina Mohan

I have been learning Carnatic Classical music, both Vocal and Violin, from 12 years and for the past 10 years, have been getting guidance and tutelage under Vid V Nalina Mohan , who is a noted violinist from Karnataka. She won a Govt. of India cultural scholarship for advanced training in violin. She has got the award "Season's best violinist" by the Gayana Samaja, Bangalore. She also received the Parur Sundaram Iyer Endowment award by Sri Krishna Gana Sabha. She is an accomplished vocalist as well.


Smt. Vinutha Prasad

To describe my mother would be to write about a hurricane in its perfect power. My mother, Smt Vinutha Prasad is a compassionate and a motivating assiduous mother. I love music and this is the skill I picked from my mother who is a delightful musician.

ABOUT

Namasthe! I am Hrishitha Kedage. Aged 15, I am a Carnatic Vocalist and a Violinist, who has been practicing music for the past 12years. I am currently being tutored by Gana Kalashree Vid. V Nalina Mohan in both Vocal and Violin.
